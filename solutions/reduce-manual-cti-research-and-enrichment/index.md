### Overview

Manual CTI research usually means repeating the same object lookups, context gathering, and cross-referencing work across many tasks.

CTI Butler reduces that repetition by giving teams one place to retrieve structured CTI context and export it into surrounding workflows.

### What This Supports

- Less time spent navigating multiple framework sites
- More consistent enrichment for reports, detections, and internal knowledge
- Faster access to real-world context linked to core CTI objects

### Why Teams Use CTI Butler for This

CTI Butler combines structured knowledge-base access with linked reports, blog posts, vulnerabilities, and export paths.

That makes it easier to enrich analyst work without rebuilding the same context from scratch each time.

### Example Outcomes

- Faster annotation of reports and findings
- Less repetitive CTI lookups in daily analyst workflow
- Better consistency in how teams reference frameworks and related material
