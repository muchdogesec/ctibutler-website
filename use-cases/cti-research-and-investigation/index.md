### Overview

This use case is for analysts who spend time looking up techniques, attack patterns, weaknesses, or related material while researching a threat, report, incident, or customer question.

CTI Butler helps compress that work into a single workflow.

### What This Workflow Looks Like

- Search for a technique, weakness, attack pattern, or other CTI object
- Follow linked context across frameworks and supporting material
- Retrieve the relevant structured data for annotation or export

### Why CTI Butler Fits

CTI Butler reduces the friction of switching between separate knowledge-base websites and separate context sources.

It is designed to make CTI research faster, clearer, and more reusable in the rest of the investigation.

### Example Outputs

- Faster analyst answers
- Better context in investigations and writeups
- More consistent references to standard CTI frameworks
