### Overview

This use case is for teams that need to annotate or enrich reports with structured CTI context instead of leaving important references buried in prose.

CTI Butler helps connect written intelligence to standard frameworks and linked supporting material.

### What This Workflow Looks Like

- Identify a technique, weakness, attack pattern, or related concept in a report
- Look up the relevant CTI object and connected context
- Add clearer framework references and supporting material to the report or dataset
- Export structured outputs when downstream tools need them

### Why CTI Butler Fits

CTI Butler gives teams one place to ground annotations in common CTI knowledge bases and reuse that context elsewhere.

That makes enrichment more consistent and easier to operationalise later.

### Example Outputs

- Better-linked analyst reports
- Faster enrichment of customer-facing intelligence
- Cleaner reuse of CTI context in follow-on tooling
